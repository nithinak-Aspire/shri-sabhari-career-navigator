# Shri Sabhari Career Navigator — Project Instructions

## Objective
Build and refine a premium, self-explanatory career-level navigator for Shri Sabhari Smelters Pvt Ltd. The output must be understandable without a presenter and should work both as an interactive web experience and a printable 16:9 PDF.

## Brand hierarchy
- Shri Sabhari is the primary organisation identity.
- Aspire is the secondary framework/design partner.
- Keep the official logos in `assets/` unchanged; do not redraw or recolour them.

## Brand palette
- Aspire Blue: `#2F66C5`
- Aspire Dark Blue: `#17458F`
- Shri Sabhari Teal: `#1BA7B8`
- Shri Sabhari Red: `#EF2526`
- Ink: `#242323`
- Cream: `#F7F1E5`

## Career-level palette — do not change
- CL1 Enterprise & Strategic Leadership: `#0A2B54`
- CL2 Function & Department Leadership: `#1F603C`
- CL3 Frontline Leadership & Senior Specialists: `#C77B2B`
- CL4 Skilled Execution & Associates: `#A9CDE5`

## UX rules
- The framework must be understandable within 30 seconds.
- Prefer visual hierarchy, pathways, and short explanatory phrases over tables.
- Keep all employee-facing wording in plain English.
- Never imply that promotion is automatic or tenure-based.
- Show vertical, specialist, and lateral growth as distinct pathways.
- Preserve keyboard navigation and print/PDF export.
- Maintain responsive behaviour for desktop and mobile.

## Technical rules
- This is a dependency-free HTML/CSS/JavaScript project.
- Do not introduce a framework unless explicitly requested.
- Keep content data-driven in `app.js` where practical.
- Test after every meaningful change by opening `index.html` through a local server.
- Do not remove print styles.

## Review checklist
Before completing any design change, verify:
1. Shri Sabhari remains primary and Aspire secondary.
2. CL1–CL4 colours are consistent everywhere.
3. No dense paragraphs or tiny text.
4. Every slide has one clear message.
5. The page works at 1440×900 and prints in 16:9.
6. Navigation controls, keyboard arrows, and interactive cards still work.
